Scene 1

Today, Ben woke up feeling a little different. He decided he would do something wonderful.

Scene 2

“Boo hoo...” Who’s crying over there? Ben stopped and listened carefully.

Scene 3

It was a lost little kitten! Ben gently said, “Don’t worry. I’ll help you find your home.”

Scene 4

“Have you seen this kitten?” Ben didn’t give up. He asked everyone he could.

Scene 5

“Don’t bother me, kid!” Ben felt a bit sad, but he didn’t stop trying.

Scene 6

“I think I saw this kitten’s owner near that tree over there.” Ben thanked her and ran off with hope in his heart.

Scene 7

“Mimi! Where did you go?” She looked at Ben and said, “Thank you so much!”

Scene 8

They became friends. Sometimes, a little courage and kindness is all it takes.

Scene 9

Ben smiled. Today, he really did something wonderful.

Scene 10

Helping others made Ben’s heart feel light as air. Tomorrow, he would keep being brave and kind.
